Time Stopper

Are you running behind on your work? Do you have dozens of emails to respond to but don't have time because you haven't finished dousing the fifty other fires that you haven't gotten to yet? Have you ever wished that you could push a button and literally stop time? Now you can.

Time Stopper is a ground-breaking app that allows you to stop the flow of time by pushing a button on your mobile device. It accomplishes this by bending the flow of chroniton radiation into a spin-lock. Being able to step outside of time and into Eternity should help you get caught up on your deliverables.

Available for Android.

Invisa-Me

Are your coworkers constantly walking up to your desk to make small talk? Is your cubicle next to the dreaded breakroom, so people say "hi" every time they make coffee? Don't you wish you could just make yourself invisible for a while so you can get things done? Now you can.

Invisa-Me is the revolutionary new technology that allows you to render yourself invisible to most of the light spectrum. It accomplishes this using proprietary Romulan technology. Note that although people can't see you, they may be able to smell you, so this won't work for certain breeds of dogs.

Now available for Android. iOS app coming soon!
